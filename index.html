Singapore Maritime University

A university for aspiring maritime students, who love to sail, and set up business in the ocean

Why SMU?

Singapore Maritime University is globally recognized and ranked Singapore's top universiy in the marine industry, as well as 7th best university globally. Students are coached with the best technical experience, nowhere away from reality. Moreover SMU's strong industry connections enable students to gain internships and job placements with prestigious shipping companies and maritime organzations. These valuable internship opportunities allow students to apply classroom knowledge, gain industry insights, and establish professional networks. Embrace the ocean's call and embark on this voyage of learning at the Singapore Maritime Academy (SMU).
